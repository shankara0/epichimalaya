About Us

Welcome to Epic Himalaya, your trusted companion for unforgettable journeys across India’s diverse destinations. Founded by passionate, adventurers and travel expert - Mr. Ashit Kumar, we specialize in trekking and travel experiences that rejuvenate. Our custom itineraries blend adrenaline‑pumping adventure with moments of serene reflection—whether camping under the Milky Way or sharing chai with a Himalayan family, every moment is uniquely yours.

At Epic Himalaya, we believe every journey should be epic. From the Himalayas to Kerala’s backwaters, we design treks and tours that are thrilling and enriching. Our knowledgeable guides and meticulous planning ensure lifelong memories and a deeper appreciation of India’s natural wonders and vibrant cultures. Your trip also supports grassroots conservation and cultural preservation.

Safety is our top priority: we provide state‑of‑the‑art gear, 24/7 support, and small group sizes for personalized attention. Whether you’re a first‑time trekker or a seasoned mountaineer, you’ll feel supported to tackle each challenge and savor every vista.

Whether you seek a challenging Himalayan climb, a peaceful yoga retreat, or a culturally immersive tour through Rajasthan’s forts, Epic Himalaya is dedicated to turning your travel dreams into reality. Join us and experience India like never before—epic journeys and once‑in‑a‑lifetime experiences await!
